# Syllabus Tracker for AI Engineer

## Overview

**Created with AI** ✨

This is an AI-powered syllabus tracker website designed to help AI engineering students stay organized and focused on their learning goals. This project was created with the assistance of artificial intelligence to provide an intelligent, user-friendly platform for managing your educational journey.

## Purpose

As an AI engineer student, tracking your syllabus can be overwhelming with numerous topics, projects, and deadlines to manage. This website solves that problem by providing:

- **Centralized Tracking**: Keep all your syllabus items, topics, and learning objectives in one organized place
- **Progress Monitoring**: Track what you've completed and what still needs to be done
- **Focus Management**: Stay focused on what matters by breaking down your syllabus into manageable tasks
- **Learning Path**: Visualize your learning journey and understand what you should be learning at each stage

## Key Features

- 📚 **Syllabus Organization**: Organize courses, subjects, and topics in a structured format
- ✅ **Task Tracking**: Mark tasks and topics as completed
- 📅 **Timeline Management**: Keep track of deadlines and important dates
- 🎯 **Goal Setting**: Set learning goals and milestones for your AI engineering education
- 📊 **Progress Visualization**: See your learning progress at a glance
- 🤖 **AI-Assisted**: Built with AI to provide smart recommendations and insights

## How It Helps

This website helps you:

1. **Stay Organized**: Never lose track of what topics you need to cover
2. **Focus Better**: Know exactly what you should be studying at any given time
3. **Track Progress**: Visualize how much you've learned and what's remaining
4. **Plan Effectively**: Better plan your study schedule with clear milestones
5. **Avoid Overwhelm**: Break down complex syllabuses into manageable pieces
